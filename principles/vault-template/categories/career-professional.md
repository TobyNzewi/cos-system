# Career & Professional

How I show up at work — with peers, clients, partners — and the posture I bring to effort, time, and the trade-offs between what's asked of me and what I'm called to.

## Prompts for the discovery session

- What do I owe my employer / partners / clients beyond the contract?
- When the work is invisible, what keeps me at full effort?
- How do I decide between executing what's asked and originating what's not?
- What's my rule for taking credit vs. giving it?
- When do I push back, and how?
- What's the line between ambition and ego in how I pursue advancement?
