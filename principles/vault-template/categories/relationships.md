# Relationships

Family, partnership, friendship, mentorship. How I show up to the people who know me, and the people I'm choosing to know.

Relationships principles are upstream of how time gets allocated in any given week. Without them, time defaults to whoever is loudest.

## Prompts for the discovery session

- Who do I owe regular contact to — and what does "regular" mean for each?
- What's my rule for showing up when someone is in distress vs. respecting their space?
- How do I decide between time at work and time with people I love?
- What do I refuse to compromise on in a partnership?
- When a friendship is drifting, what do I do?
- What's my rule for keeping promises to people, especially small ones?
