# Personal Growth

How I think about my own development. What I'm willing to pay (in discomfort, time, pride) to grow. What I refuse to do in the name of growth.

This is where Dalio's framework lands most directly — pain + reflection = progress, ego and blind spots, the 5-step process. Test his framework against your own experience — including patterns of avoidance.

## Prompts for the discovery session

- What's my rule for handling pain — physical, emotional, professional?
- When I notice I'm avoiding something, what's the response?
- How do I decide what feedback to take seriously vs. let pass?
- What's the difference between productive struggle and spinning my wheels?
- What am I willing to be bad at in public in order to get good at it?
- What growth promises do I make to myself that I keep breaking — and what does that mean?
