# Faith & Spirituality

What I believe, how I practice, and the deeper questions about meaning, purpose, and the source of values.

This category is the foundation under the others. Principles here will likely show up echoed or contradicted in other categories — that contrast is the point.

If you're not religious, this category still applies — it's about the deepest layer of "what is this for" that informs everything else, whatever frame you hold it in.

## Prompts for the discovery session

- What do I actually believe — not what I was raised to say?
- What's my rule for spiritual / contemplative practice — what makes a day "with" vs. "without"?
- When fear or pride shows up, what's the test I run before acting?
- What text, teaching, or thinker do I keep returning to, and why?
- When my career and my deepest commitments pull in different directions, what's the tiebreaker?
- What's the difference between bold action and ego-driven aggression in my actual behavior?
