# Health & Wellness

The body, sleep, exercise, mental health. The category that gets squeezed first when work or travel intensifies — which is exactly why it needs principles, not just goals.

## Prompts for the discovery session

- What's my non-negotiable on sleep, and what triggers exceptions?
- What movement do I owe my body each week, and what's the minimum?
- What's my rule on alcohol, caffeine, and other substances I consume regularly?
- When I notice I'm drifting on health, what's the response?
- What does "rest" actually mean for me — and how do I distinguish it from avoidance?
- How do I take care of my mental health when work is heavy?
